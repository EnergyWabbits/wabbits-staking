# 🏦 Energy Wabbits — Treasury System

## Overview

The Energy Wabbits treasury is designed to create a **self-sustaining ecosystem**.

Instead of relying on constant new users, the system generates value internally through activity, fees, and engagement.

---

## 💸 Treasury Inflows (How Money Comes In)

The treasury is funded through key actions across the ecosystem:

### Core Sources:

- WB Claim Fees  
- Raffle Ticket Purchases  
- Casino & Game Activity  
- Boost Pack Purchases  
- Shop-related actions (if applied)  

Each action contributes a small amount that builds over time.

---

## 🔁 Example Flow

1. Players earn WB  
2. Players claim WB (fee applied)  
3. Players use WB in games and raffles  
4. Activity generates additional fees  
5. Fees flow into the treasury  

---

## 🎯 Purpose of the Treasury

The treasury exists to:

- Fund rewards  
- Support prize pools  
- Maintain long-term sustainability  
- Support development and growth  

---

## 🎮 Treasury + Games

Games are a major contributor to treasury flow.

### Example:

- Slots spins generate small fees  
- Raffles collect WB  
- Casino activity cycles value  

This keeps money circulating instead of leaving the system.

---

## ⚖️ Balance Design

The treasury is designed to balance:

- Rewards going out  
- Value coming in  

Without this balance, the system would not last long-term.

---

## 🚀 Sustainability Model

Energy Wabbits is built around:

- Continuous player activity  
- Small recurring fees  
- Engaged community participation  

This allows the ecosystem to grow **organically over time**.

---

## 📊 Why This Matters

A strong treasury:

- Supports long-term rewards  
- Reduces reliance on hype  
- Builds real ecosystem strength  
- Creates trust with users  

---

## 🔁 Simple Breakdown

Players → Earn → Spend → Fees → Treasury → Rewards → Players
